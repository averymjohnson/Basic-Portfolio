While working at CTG, I obtained my Series 3 to become a licenses broker in futures and commodities. After a year there, I returned to the Kansas City area and started working for State Street Corporation. Where I price international mutual funds, based out of Luxemboug and Ireland, for clients UBS and Deutsche Bank.
